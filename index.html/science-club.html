Nusrat Science and Maths Club

About the Science Club

The Science Club is dedicated to exploring the wonders of the scientific world. Our members engage in experiments, explore theories, and discuss recent scientific discoveries to enhance our understanding of science.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To inspire and engage students in the fields of science and mathematics through hands-on experiments, collaborative learning, and exploration of real-world applications.

Vision: To foster a community of curious minds who are passionate about science and mathematics, preparing them for future challenges and innovations.

Club Activities

  • Conduct weekly science experiments
  • Host guest lectures from renowned scientists
  • Organize field trips to science museums and research centers
  • Participate in national science fairs and competitions
