Press Club

About the Press Club

The Press Club is a platform for students passionate about journalism, writing, and media. We engage in various activities that promote critical thinking, creativity, and effective communication, while covering school events and news stories.

Club Activities

  • Write articles for the school newsletter
  • Conduct interviews with students and staff
  • Attend workshops on journalism and media ethics
  • Organize and cover school events and news stories
